Our Story

Sweet Dreams Bakery has been serving the Kahului community for over 25 years, bringing European baking traditions to beautiful Maui.

Founded by master baker Elena Rodriguez in 1999, we've built our reputation on quality ingredients, traditional methods, and genuine aloha spirit. Every item is crafted by hand using recipes passed down through generations.
